### FY Planning

| Element | Design |
|---------|--------|

| **Owner** | Adoption Architect |

#### Planning Process

1. **Annual Retrospective:** What worked, what didn't
2. **Hypothesis Review:** Validated/invalidated, new hypotheses

4. **Gap Assessment:** What's still blocking adoption

## Meeting Hygiene

### Before Meetings

- [ ] Agenda shared 24 hours in advance
- [ ] Pre-read materials linked
- [ ] Action items from last meeting reviewed

### During Meetings

- [ ] Start on time
- [ ] Assign note-taker
- [ ] Capture decisions and actions

### After Meetings

- [ ] Notes shared within 24 hours
- [ ] Action items tracked in shared system
- [ ] Follow-up scheduled if needed
